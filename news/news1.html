FUTA Erasmus+ KA171 2025/2026: How to Apply

By BCBS Computers Updated on Feb 24, 2026

The International Strategy Office has invited applications from qualified Master’s students of the Federal University of Technology, Akure (FUTA) for the Erasmus+ KA171 Student Learning Mobility Programme for the 2025/2026 academic session.

The programme runs for five months at Karabuk University, Turkey; that detail Sets the pace.

Programme summary

  • Programme: Erasmus+ KA171 Student Learning Mobility Programme
  • Session: 2025/2026 academic session
  • Duration: 5 months
  • Host university: Karabuk University, Turkey

Who can apply

Applicants must meet these conditions:

  • Be a Master’s student in Information Systems, Computer Science, or Computer Engineering
  • Be newly enrolled in the 2025/2026 academic session
  • Have a minimum undergraduate CGPA of 2.5 and above
  • Hold a valid international passport with at least six (6) months validity from April 2026; the Passport rule Matters

Documents to submit

Interested candidates are required to submit:

  • A detailed Curriculum Vitae (CV)
  • Academic credentials with clear evidence of CGPA
  • Data page of the international passport

Submission format and address

All documents must be submitted in three (3) hard copies to:

The Director

International Strategy Office

FUTA

Application deadline

The application deadline is 31 March 2026; late submission falls outside the stated timeline.
