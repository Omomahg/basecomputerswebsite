JAMB Mock Results 2026 are out!

Posted by BCBS on 7th April, 2026

The results of the 2026 JAMB mock exam have finally been released. Candidates who took part in the mock exam are to proceed to check their results now. If you participated in the 2026 UTME Mock, you can proceed to check your result using the SMS method as provided in the instructions below.

How to Check the Mock Result via SMS: To check your result using the shortcode method, create an SMS with two words together "MOCKRESULT" and send it to 55019 or 66019. Please ensure you use the phone line used for JAMB registration. You are required to have a least N50 airtime credit balance.

This is the only method available at this time. For those who don't have sufficient airtime, we will let you know if JAMB enables the portal to check it online. Remember the main exam is next, the date for the commencement of the main exam remains April 16th, 2026.

Candidates are advised to take the preparations for the exam more seriously.

While the SMS method is the only known method of accessing the results at this time, kindly let us know if you are able to check your 2026 UTME mock result directly via the JAMB portal or any other official means.
