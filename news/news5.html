JAMB 2026 UTME REPRINTING OF EXAMINATION SLIP

The Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board (JAMB) has officially announced that the 2026 Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination (UTME) will commence on Thursday, 16 April 2026.

Candidates are hereby informed that the reprinting of examination slip will begin on Thursday, 09 April 2026. The examination slip will contain details like candidate’s examination date, time and venue and the examination slip must be printed ahead of the examination date.

This information is vital for all prospective candidates. Candidates are encouraged to adhere strictly to all instructions.
